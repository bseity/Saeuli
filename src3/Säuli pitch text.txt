Säuli

Säuli (swissgerman “Soili” = little piglet)
In 2020, 722’000 people in Switzerland were affected by poverty. -Caritas

The aim of the Säuli is not only to provide a little extra relief to those affected, but also as a comfort feature for all people.
Säuli works as a digital collective piggy bank that can be managed by the shop owner (Coop). 
As a customer, you have a possibility to donate or let Säuli cover your small shortage to pay for your groceries.

You can be assured that whenever you give your spare change to Säuli you and society can benefit. 
High security protection can be implemented to prevent abuse of the system.

Säuli is fair: Everyone can take part regardless of socio economic status.

Säuli can make the difference: Makes charity easy. Eliminates Stigma. Provides peace of mind. 

Little step – big impact: Even 5 cents make the difference.

Säuli cares: Säuli reduces workload of staff members, spares time consuming cancelations, returns of products on their shelfs and even food waste. 

Benefit for the shop owner: Säuli could be fitted with such lucrative additional functions like pairing with the store benefit point system (Supercard),
integration in Twint and other payment systems, doner loyalty benefits and more. This gives additional value, allows an even better customer experience.
